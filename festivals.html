Fall

Downtown Harvest Festival

  • When: Third Saturday of October
  • Cost: No admission fee
  • Free activities spread across Fountain Square, Circus Square, SoKY Center, The Fringe, and Heritage Trail
  • Games and activities, live music, local vendors, food trucks
  • Infamous pumpkin drop at the top of every hour with the BG Fire Department
Go to Website

Bowling Green International Festival

  • When: Last Saturday of September
  • Cost:
    • $5 in advance and $10 at the door
    • 12 and under are free
    • With EBT card - up to 2 tickets for $2 each
  • Celebrates the culture and heritage of Bowling Green Residents
  • Has music, dance, demonstrations, authentic foreign foods, activities, cultural displays and an international bazaar
  • Can volunteer or participate (booths, performances, teach lessons)
Go to Website

Bourbon & Brewfest

  • When: End of October (October 25, 2025)
  • Cost: General admission - $50
  • 21 and older
  • Celebration of state breweries and distilleries
  • Wide variety of craft distilleries and breweries
  • Shop from local vendors
  • Special VIP Experiences available
Go to Website

Spring

White Squirrel Arts Fest

  • When: Every April (April 25, 2026)
  • Cost: Free
  • In historic Shake Rag District
  • Showcases local art talent
  • entertainment, arts activities, artist and author booths, readings, and vendors
  • fashion show at White Squirrel Brewery, a Theatrical Reading at Fountain Square Players, and evening Poetry Slam at Shake Rag Bar & Lounge
Go to Website

Summer

Duncan Hines Days

  • When: Every June for a Week
  • Cost: Free
  • In honor of Duncan Hines – born and raised in Bowling Green; WKU alum; traveling salesman turned food and travel guidebook publisher
  • Different activities every day of the week
  • Culminates with Eats & Beats Music Festival
