The Pots Place Gallery

  • eight potters work and sell on site in the Gallery
  • offers jewelry, scarves, wooden bowls & toys, pottery, wall art, and other artwork by local Kentucky artists
  • Pottery classes available
  • Open Tues.-Sat. from 11am-5pm

Southern Kentucky Performing Arts Center

  • M-F 10am-5pm; Ticket Office opens 2 hours prior to any Saturday or Sunday event
  • 1,800-seat state-of-the-art auditorium
  • Broadway musicals, family entertainment, concerts, comedians and original theatrical productions
  • Home of Orchestra Kentucky, Ramsey Theatre Company and BG OnStage
  • https://www.theskypac.com/

Downing Museum

  • Open Tuesday-Saturday 10am to 4pm; Closed all holidays and occasional Saturdays for private events M-F 10am-5pm; Ticket Office opens 2 hours prior to any Saturday or Sunday event
  • displays a collection of fine art by the late Joe Downing
  • Also has works by regional artists
  • https://www.jerryebakerfoundation.org/downing-museum/

The Symphony at WKU

  • cooperative between Western Kentucky University's Department of Music and the Symphony Association
  • Comprised of WKU students and faculty members, and musicians from the surrounding region
  • https://www.wku.edu/music/symphony.php
